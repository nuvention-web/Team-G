Home Chef

Tired of your own cooking? Tired of eating out all the time? Home Chef brings people who love to cook close to people who need their fix of home cooked food. You don't have to have the same whole pan of lasagna for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Use Home Chef to see what the city has to offer. You'd be surprised at the culinary talent you could find in your own neighborhood!

Want to have your own small home bakery? Feel like making a profit by whippping up some excellent food? How about showing off the hidden chef inside you? Use Home Chef to put up all that you can make and share. Now who does not love a good portion of home made food!

Current Focus

Analyzing and gaining a better perspective of our user space is the current focus of the team. Eliciting required information from prospective users will help in building a system that is more aware of the user's needs. We are also brainstorming on all the functionalities the system should support in tandem.

Team's Mission

Through the course, we wish to develop and bring a product to market, learn all the pitfalls involved in entrepreneurship along the way and hopefully have a successful business up and running in the future. We would also like to network and build connections so as to leverage them in any future ventures that we might embark on.

Product Space

Our product spaces include home cooked food and perhaps food delivery services. We're looking for a way to commodify home cooked food within a community. Depending on insights gained from interviews, we might look into incorporating a delivery service too.

Our main customers are people who cook at home, and people who want the convenience of take-out and the satisfaction of a tasty, healthy meal. The community in Evanston would be our Beachhead market. Of course, depending on the insights we gain from our interviews about customer priorities, our customer focus might shift.
